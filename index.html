About Volt International

We are a leading provider of construction supplies in Glendale, CA, serving contractors and builders with top-quality materials since 2020.

Building Excellence Since 2020

Volt International LLC has been a trusted name in the construction industry, providing premium building materials to contractors, builders, and DIY enthusiasts across Southern California.

Our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer service has made us the go-to supplier for construction projects of all sizes. From small home renovations to large commercial developments, we have the materials and expertise to support your project.

With our extensive inventory and knowledgeable staff, we can help you find exactly what you need to complete your project on time and within budget.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of construction supplies and services to meet all your building needs.

Cement & Concrete

High-quality cement, ready-mix concrete, and concrete additives for all your construction needs. We carry all major brands and types for any project specification.

Lumber & Wood Products

Premium lumber, plywood, and engineered wood products for framing, finishing, and all construction applications. Pressure-treated and specialty woods available.

Steel & Rebar

Structural steel, rebar, and metal products for reinforced concrete construction. We stock standard sizes and can order custom lengths and specifications.

Bricks & Masonry

Wide selection of bricks, blocks, stones, and masonry supplies for beautiful, durable construction. We carry both structural and decorative options.

Tools & Equipment

Professional-grade construction tools and equipment from top brands. Hand tools, power tools, safety gear, and equipment rentals available.

Safety Equipment

Comprehensive range of safety equipment including helmets, gloves, harnesses, and protective clothing to keep your job site OSHA-compliant and safe.
